ANIMAL FARM

Book cover redesign - Editorial project

The objective of this project was to redesign the cover and back cover of a book of choice. The chosen book, Animal Farm by George Orwell, narrates a political allegory with animals, exploring themes of power, corruption, and betrayal. I aimed to represent these social ideas using vibrant colors, typography disrupted with disorder, and objects that are representative of the novel.

About me

Hi, I'm Silvi. As a Digital Graphic Design student at Da Vinci School, I am focusing on developing competence to enter the job market, with a special interest in the area of user experience and interface design. I have certifications in UX-UI design and Front-end development. Additionally, I have knowledge in a variety of tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects, Lightroom, Canva, and Figma.

During my time in Spain, I worked as a web developer, obtained a professional certification in Robotic Process Automation (RPA), and participated in the 42 school, where I acquired fundamental programming knowledge using Git and C. I have also skills in tools such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL, C, and .NET.
